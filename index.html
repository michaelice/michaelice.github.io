About

  • Currently, I am a member of the Hangzhou Artificial Intelligence Society and serve as a Large Language Model (LLM) Algorithm Expert at Zhipu Huazhang, where I lead the Intelligent Agent Algorithm team in the Broad-Internet Division, focusing on AI Agents, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and multimodal technologies.
  • Previously, I was the Algorithm Director at Fushu Technology, Algorithm Expert at Wacai, Beibei Group, and Tencent, as well as an R&D Engineer at Sunyard and a core contributor to the Yitao R&D promotion system. I have also received algorithm expert offers from top companies such as Didi Chuxing, Youzan, and JD.com.
  • I have pioneered privacy-computing algorithms and platforms that have won industry awards and achieved real-world deployments. My research interests include reinforcement learning, privacy computing, natural language processing (NLP), distributed machine learning, and graph computing.
  • Educational background in Computer Science, with primary supervision from Professor Mingsheng Shang at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), and additional mentorship from Professors Xiaopu Han, Zike Zhang, and Linyuan Lü — now Ph.D. advisors at Zhejiang University and the University of Science and Technology of China.In 2014, participated as a visiting student at Beijing Normal University, under the supervision of Professor Wenxu Wang, focusing on complex networks and network reconstruction.Completed secondary education at Jinhua No.1 High School in Zhejiang Province, Science track, with a top-tier entrance score exceeding the national elite university admission thresholds.
